- Thief Enhancement Pack -
Release 2
Friday the 1st of January, 2020.

What is it?

This project is an effort to improve the quality of the graphics in Thief 2: The Metal Age, and also in Thief: The Dark Project and Thief Gold.
The ultimate goal is to replace all the old, low-quality objects and textures from Thief 1 and 2 with versions that have higher polygon counts and texture resolutions, while keeping as close to the originals as possible.

Installation instructions:

The Enhancement Pack requires NewDark 1.21 or higher, and is compatible with Thief 2: The Metal Age, Thief: The Dark Project and Thief Gold.

To install the Enhancement Pack, simply drop ep2.zip into your Thief installation's "mods" directory, and add it to the mod_path in cam_mod.ini

For Thief 2, this should look like this: mod_path mods\ep2

For Thief 1, you should instead use: mod_path mods\ep2\thief1+mods\ep2

If you are installing to Thief 1, open Dark.cfg and make sure that both the lines ObjTextures16 and MeshTextures16 are somewhere in that file, on a line of their own. Also make sure that there is one empty line at the end of the file.

 

Uninstallation

To uninstall the Enhancement Pack, simply delete ep2.zip. You may also want to remove references to it from your cam_mod.ini.
